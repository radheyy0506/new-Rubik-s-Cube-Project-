Solving puzzles, building futures.

Hi, I’m Radhika Ravindra Janawekar, a second-year B.Tech student in Computer Science Engineering (Cyber Security specialization) at Finolex Academy of Management and Technology, Ratnagiri.

I have been practicing the Rubik’s Cube for over 6 years, gaining experience in solving multiple modes, shapes, and multi-layer cubes. What started as a curiosity gradually turned into a deep passion.

However, at the beginning of my journey, I faced many challenges—especially in understanding the solving methods and finding the right guidance. This struggle became my motivation.

I created this website to provide a platform where beginners and enthusiasts can learn, practice, and track their progress in solving the Rubik’s Cube. My goal is to make learning simpler, more interactive, and accessible for everyone.
