CUBE BASICS

The Rubik’s Cube is a 3D mechanical puzzle invented in 1974 by Ernő Rubik. Originally a tool for geometry, it is now the world's most famous challenge.

🎯 Purpose of Learning

  • 🚀 Improves memory & concentration
  • 🧠 Develops logical thinking
  • 🛠️ Enhances problem-solving skills
  • ⏱️ Builds patience & speed

🎨 Colors & Standard Rules

Standard cubes have 6 fixed colors. Use the Opposite Rules to orient yourself:

White
Yellow
Red
Orange
Blue
Green

⚠️ Remember: White ↔ Yellow | Red ↔ Orange | Blue ↔ Green

🧱 Structure of the Cube

1. Corners (3 Colors): 8 pieces. Only orientation changes.

2. Edges (2 Colors): Located between corners. In 4x4+, these must be paired.

3. Centers (1 Color): Define the face color. HPCs (Helper Pieces) on big cubes.
