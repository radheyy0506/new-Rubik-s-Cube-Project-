LAYER BY LAYER

1st Layer 2nd Layer 3rd Layer
01 Goal: Create White +

🟩 STEP 1: White Cross

Align white edges with their corresponding side center colors.

LOGIC BASED (No Fixed Algo)

✔️ Look for "daisy" pattern if you get stuck!

02 Goal: Complete First Layer

🟩 STEP 2: White Corners

Place corner pieces. Keep the target corner at the Bottom-Right.

R' D' R D

📌 Repeat until the corner piece is oriented correctly.

03 Goal: Middle Edges

🟦 STEP 3: Middle Layer

Case 1: Move to RIGHT

U R U' R' U' F' U F

Case 2: Move to LEFT

U' L' U L U F U' F'
04 Goal: Top Cross

🟨 STEP 4: Yellow Cross

Create the yellow "+" pattern on the top face.

F R U R' U' F'

📌 Sequence: Dot → L-Shape → Line → Cross

05 Goal: Full Yellow Face

🟨 STEP 5: Yellow Corners

Make the entire top face yellow (Sune algorithm).

R U R' U R U2 R'
06 Goal: Align Corners

🔺 STEP 6: Position Corners

Place top corners in their correct geometric positions.

U R U' L' U R' U' L
07 Goal: Finish Cube

🔁 STEP 7: Final Edges

Rotate the remaining edges to complete the puzzle.

F2 U L R' F2 L' R U F2

Master Tips

  • 📍 Orientation: Always keep White at the bottom after Step 2.
  • 🐢 Speed: Move slowly! Accuracy is more important than speed when learning.
  • 🔄 Notation: ' means Anti-clockwise. R2 means turn the face twice.

“In the Layer-by-Layer method, we first solve the white layer, then the middle layer, and finally the yellow layer using specific algorithms. This method is simple and best for beginners to understand the structure of the cube.”
